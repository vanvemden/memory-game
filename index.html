Here's how to play:

  1. Select a number of cards. More cards builds more brainpower.
  2. Enter at least one brain's name. Up to four brains can play.
  3. Winner gets bragging rights, or enter another prize.
  4. Click the Start button
  5. Then I'll build, mix, and lay the custom cards.
  6. First brain flips 2 cards. If they match; go again.
  7. If cards don't match, they will turn back over in 3 seconds.
  8. In case of multibrain game, next brain flips 2 cards.
  9. Game is over when all card have been matched.
  10. Brain with most matches in least clicks wins.
