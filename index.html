How to play

  1. Select a number of cards; more cards builds more brainpower.
  2. Enter a name for brain 1. Up to 4 brains can play.
  3. Click the Play button, and wait for the cards.
  4. First brain flips 2 cards. If they match; go again.
  5. If cards do not match, they'll turn back over after 3 seconds.
  6. In case of multibrain game, next player flips 2 cards.
  7. Game is over when all card have been matched.
  8. The brain with most matches in least clicks wins!
